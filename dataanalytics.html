China's AQI Project

Step 1: Data Cleaning

The cleaning process involved getting an overview of the data and columns of the dataset as well as the removal of any missing or out-ofrange values that could affect the accuracy of the analysis. I used SAS programming to clean the data and perform data transformation on the AQI dataset of the 4 major Chinese cities.

Step 2: Descriptive Analysis

This process involved a complete description of the data and columns of the dataset using Tableau with a focus on the trends that the AQI levels saw over the years between 2015 and 2021 in all 4 cities.

Step 3: Inferential Analysis

This process consisted of the hyothesis testing and the analysis of the correlation between the Covid deaths and the AQI levels data using SAS software. Based on the results of this analysis, I concluded that the AQI levels and Covid deaths had no correlation and therefore, we cannot say that one of them affected the other.
